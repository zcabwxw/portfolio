How to wash your hands

  • Wet your hands with water and apply soap.
  • Rub your hands and scrub the backs of your hands, between your fingers, and under your nails.
  • Continue rubbing for at least 20 seconds.
  • Rinse your hands.
  • Dry your hands using a clean towel.4
