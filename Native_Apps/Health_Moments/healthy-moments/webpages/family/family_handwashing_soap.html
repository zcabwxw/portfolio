Soap

  • Bar soap should not sit in a dish of water. Germs may grow and spread from one person to another. Ensure no one in the home has a skin infection, and rinse soap well before and after using it.
  • Antibacterial soap is not recommended; it provides no extra health benefits over soap and water.6
