Hand sanitizers

  • Adults can use hand sanitizers if soap and running water are not available and hands are not visibly dirty.
  • Children under 2 should NEVER use hand sanitizers.
  • Children over 2 years old can ONLY use hand sanitizers with close adult supervision to ensure the children use it correctly, and do not eat it or rub it into their eyes.7

How to use hand sanitizers

  • Apply the product to the hands to wet all surfaces of both hands.
  • Rub your hands together.
  • Rub the product over all surfaces of your hands and fingers until your hands are dry.
  • Use a product that is 60% alcohol-based or more.8
  • Always store hand sanitizers out of reach and sight of children. They are poisonous and flammable.
