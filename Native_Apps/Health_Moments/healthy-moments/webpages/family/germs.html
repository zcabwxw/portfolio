How this relates to germs

  • Your hands pick up germs from all of the things they touch and then spread them from one place to another.
  • Germs can enter your body when you eat, touch your eyes, nose, mouth, or broken skin.
  • Handwashing with soap and water removes visible dirt and hidden germs.
  • Handwashing reduced the number of diarrheal illnesses by 31 percent and respiratory illnesses by 21 percent.
