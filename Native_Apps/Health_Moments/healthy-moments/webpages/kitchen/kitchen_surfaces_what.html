What surfaces to wash

  • Toys/objects children put in their mouths
  • Surfaces children put their mouths on
  • Surfaces and objects touched by a child or adult who is ill
  • Surfaces that are visibly soiled
  • Surfaces used for cooking and eating
  • Surfaces touched during diapering, toileting and handwashing9
